The HTML

The scope attribute declares the direction of the header.

The caption are used to find and understand what the table is about.

The summary gives information of the data and its data.

<table summary="rows of students and their abstenteism status" >
    <caption>Student Roster</caption>
    <tr>
      <td></td>
      <th scope="col">Present</th>
      <th scope="col">Absent</th>
    </tr>
    <tr>
      <th scope="row">Sam</th>
      <td>true</td>
      <td>false</td>
    </tr>
    <tr>
      <th scope="row">Maxine</th>
      <td>false</td>
      <td>true</td>
    </tr>
  </table>
